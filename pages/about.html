Gaming Industry

PC Gaming

Known for its high-performance capabilities and modding community, PC gaming remains a robust segment, with platforms like Steam offering vast libraries of games.

Mobile Gaming

With the proliferation of smartphones, mobile gaming has surged in popularity. Titles like "Candy Crush" and "PUBG Mobile" have reached millions of users worldwide, making mobile gaming a significant revenue generator.

Esports

Competitive gaming, or esports, has emerged as a major phenomenon, drawing in huge audiences and sponsorships. Games like "League of Legends" and "Fortnite" have become staples in the esports arena.
